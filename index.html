Mahmoud Abdulsalam

Founder of Alshaht Son's PCB

Mahmoud Abdulsalam was born in Egypt on July 18th, 1994. He received his B.Sc. degree (with Honors.) in electrical engineering from Electrical Power and Machines Eng. Dept. at Tanta university, Tanta, Egypt, in 2017. Since Aug. 2017, he has been a research assistant at Power Electronics and Electric Drives research laboratory at Tanta University, where he helped in implementing several power electronic converters for different research activities. Since Dec. 2018, he has been working toward the MSc. Degree in power electronics at Tanta University in the modeling and control of modular multilevel converters with energy storage systems for photovoltaic applications. Mr. Abdulsalam is an IEEE student member, and he is also a member of the IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS) Egypt chapter. His research interests include power electronic converters topologies, electric drives systems, electric vehicle, and Photovoltaic systems. He always seeks to enhance his soft and technical skills, especially in the implementation of PCB circuits and utilizing the DSPs and Microcontroller units in the power electronics applications. Finally, Mr. Abdulsalam has more experience in both scientific research, practical skills, technical skills, soft skills, teamwork, research projects, and other research activities that can be submitted upon your request.
